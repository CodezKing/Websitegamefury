FAQ - Your Questions Answered

Find answers to common queries and get all the info you need to get started!

1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure online payment options.

2. How do I track my order after it's been shipped?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website or through the carrier's tracking system to monitor your shipment's progress.

3. How long does shipping take, and do you offer international delivery?

Shipping times vary depending on your location, but most domestic orders are delivered within 3-7 business days. We do offer international shipping, and delivery times for international orders may vary depending on your country and shipping method.

4. Can I return or exchange a game or accessory?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided the product is in its original condition. Digital products (like downloadable games) are non-returnable. Please check our full return policy for more details.

5. Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we regularly offer discounts, seasonal sales, and special promotions. Be sure to sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals and offers.
